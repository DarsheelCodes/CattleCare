Overview

Our innovative Cattle Health Monitoring System leverages advanced technology to ensure the well-being of livestock. The system uses wearable devices, such as temperature sensors and pulse oximeters, to track vital signs like body temperature and oxygen levels in real time, enabling early detection of health issues.

In addition, environmental sensors monitor temperature, humidity, and water feed management, ensuring optimal living conditions for cattle. The system also includes safety features like security alerts, fire alarms, and flood detection.

With the introduction of Cellufenu, a natural temperature and humidity regulating material, we provide a comfortable and secure environment for cattle, improving health management and farm efficiency.
